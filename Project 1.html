Great White Sharks

A Great White Shark's scientific name is Carcharodon Carcharias. They can live up to 70 years and can grow up to 20 feet. On average Great Whites usually wheigh 2.5 tons or more. thats heavier than a piano. They are the largest predatory fish on Earth. When an adult Great Whites like to eat large prey, such as: sea lions, seals, small toothed whales, otters, and sea turtles. When they eat the swallow whole, no chewing.

From 1580 to 2010 93% of shark attacks worldwide were on men. In Florida the month with the most attacks is September. Sharks normally do not hurt humans, but when they do they usually mistake the human as something else. Also, most of the time the attack is provoked by the human. More people kill sharks than sharks killing people. Sharks are vunerable and will be extinct if people don't stop harming their species.
